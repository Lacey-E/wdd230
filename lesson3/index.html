Site Discription

The purpose of this document is to describe the weathergirlnaija.com website, its purpose, audience and other aspect of it's organization, structure, style, etc.

Site Name

The site name is weathergirlnaija.com, this site will be a community for all weather lovers

The purpose

The main purpose of weathergirlnaija.com will be to create not just a 'weather' reporting site but rather a community, and a regional site that includes the idea of event promotion, relevant news. It will also allow its users to comment and write relevant content to the site. This will keep everybody together and allow everybody to write or send pictures and ask questions, it will be a weather site for weather lovers. Weathergirlnaija.com will keep its users informed, educated, and accepted.
